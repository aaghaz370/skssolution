About Us

SKS Hitech Solution LLP is a trusted provider of complete sewage and wastewater treatment solutions,
serving residential, industrial, and commercial clients with a focus on sustainability, innovation, and 24/7 customer support.

We specialize in STPs, ETPs, ZLD systems, and water recycling solutions—offering design, installation, automation, and maintenance under one roof.

Our expert team delivers efficient, eco-friendly, and fully compliant systems powered by modern technologies like remote monitoring and PLC automation.

Backed by a commitment to quality, service excellence, and environmental responsibility, we ensure seamless operations and long-term reliability with our round-the-clock technical support and AMC services.

SKS Hitech Solution LLP — clean water, clean future, anytime you need it.
